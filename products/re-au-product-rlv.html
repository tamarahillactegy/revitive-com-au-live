How to use REVITVE LV

  1. Plug the device into a power socket close to a sofa or chair
  2. Sit down and place both your bare feet on the device
  3. With your toe, press the ON button
  4. Increase the intensity by pressing the ‘+’ button, again with your toe. You will begin to feel a tingling sensation in your feet. Keep increasing the intensity until you see a slight contraction in your calf muscles
  5. Sit back and enjoy
